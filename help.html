1. Add a task
To add a task click on "Add Task" at the Navigation. Fill in the requested Information an click on "Create Task".
2. Show all tasks
To show all Tasks just click on "Board" at the Navigation.
3. Show task details
To show the details of a task just click on it.
4. Delete a task
To delete a task click on it to show the details. At the bottom of the details click on the delete icon.
5. Change task status
To change a task status click on it, hold and drag it into the desired status column.
